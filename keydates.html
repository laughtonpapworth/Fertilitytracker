Key Fertility Dates

Ovulation:
When you get a solid smiley face on the Clearblue test, ovulation is likely to occur within 12–36 hours.

Implantation Window:
Implantation typically occurs 6–10 days after ovulation. Mild spotting or cramping may happen.

Pregnancy Testing:
The most reliable results come 14 days after ovulation (DPO), but early detection tests may show results as early as 10 DPO.
