Cervical Mucus

Cervical mucus (CM) changes throughout your cycle and can help identify fertile days.

Types of CM:
• Dry: Infertile
• Sticky: Low fertility
• Creamy: Possibly fertile
• Watery: Fertile
• Egg White: Most fertile (ideal for sperm movement)

Observe daily and record the most fertile type. Egg white mucus typically appears just before ovulation.
